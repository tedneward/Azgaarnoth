Undead

The undead are those creatures brought back to some semblance of existence through necromantic or foul arts. Any "natural" creature (that is, not a celestial, fiend, elemental, or undead) can be raised/transformed as undead, either as a skeleton, a zombie, ghost, withered, or even mummified, vampiric, or into lichdom.
