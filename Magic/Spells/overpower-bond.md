**Casting Time**: 1 action

**Range**: 30 feet

**Components**: V, S

**Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 minute

This creature gains 10 temporary hit points and its attacks count as magical for the purposes of overcoming damage immunities and resistances. 

***At Higher Levels***. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the temporary hit points increase by 4 for each slot level above 2nd.
