#### Brightfield
*6th-level evocation* *(ritual)* (Bard, Cleric, Wizard)
___
- **Casting Time:** 1 action
- **Range:** Self (150-foot radius)
- **Components:** V, S, M (a lit candle, torch, or lantern)
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 hour
---
The ambient illumination in your surrounding area is augmented, providing a persistent light source across a wide range. Bright light is projected out to 150 feet, and dim light for another 150 feet from your current location. The affected area remains stationary from the location where you cast this spell.If you cast this spell as a ritual, you must use as a material component a topaz worth at least 200 gp, which is consumed by the spell.
