#### Cloak of Adeamoz
*Abjuration cantrip (Artificer,Cleric)
___
- **Casting Time:** 1 action
- **Range:** Self
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** 1 round
---
A transparent cloak wraps around you, warding you
from close magical strikes. Until the start of your
next turn, you gain resistance to damage from
melee spell attacks.
