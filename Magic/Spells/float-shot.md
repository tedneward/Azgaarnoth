#### Float Shot
*1st-level evocation* (Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard)
___
- **Casting Time:** 1 action
- **Range:** 120 feet
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Instantaneous
---

On a hit, the bullet deals 1d8 damage of the chosen type.
